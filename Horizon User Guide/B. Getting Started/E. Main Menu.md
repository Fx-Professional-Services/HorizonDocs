### What's in the Main Menu of Horizon?

The first thing you see when you're logged into Horizon is the _main menu_. Use the menus on the left side to choose commands and perform tasks in the application. 

![](6_main_menu.png)
 
 **All Parties**: Choose this menu to navigate through the records of **customers**, **vendors,** **employees,** and more. 

**Customers**: Choose this menu to manage payment transaction records. 

**Products**: Choose this menu to manage all items, which are products, services, configurators, and equipment in the system. 

A. **Products** are consumable physical items that can be created or purchased, each with prices for picking and selling, such as vendor prices.

C. **Configurators** offer customers predetermined options for selecting products, such as different types of six-inch cakes (e.g., yellow, chocolate, red velvet, carrot). Each choice results in a specific sales order item being entered, and the configurator also determines the Bill of Materials (BOM) needed to produce the chosen product.

D. Equipment refers to necessary items used in product creation and order fulfillment, ranging from kitchen utensils like plates and knives to essential items for catering social events such as tables, chairs, and cloths.
